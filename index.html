Welcome to NV Foundation

NV Foundation is a non-profit organization that provides support for children and families living with cancer.
Founded in 2020, NV Foundation's mission is to form a community of friends and supporters who provide the financial power and volunteer time that enables young adults with cancer to live life fully.
Our mission is to provide quality education to all.



Why Donate Us?

Education

The world is changing, and so are the challenges that we face. The way we teach children is no exception. Schools now need to prepare students for careers in a globalized world, where they will be working with people from all over the world.

Disaster Relief

Donating money is a great way to help those in need. You can donate through your credit card or PayPal account, or by donating goods such as clothing, non-perishable food items, and other items.

Health & Life

We need your help to continue providing health care services for the people.Donate for health care is a platform that allows you to donate money in order to improve people's lives.

Tree Plantation

TreePlantation is a platform that helps in plantation of trees on behalf of the donor. By doing so it will help to create awareness about the importance of tree planting and make people aware of their contribution towards environment.
